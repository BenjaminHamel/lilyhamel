BookMooch

Book Mooch is a free website for exchanging books with other readers. Once you list 10 books you are willing to send to someone else, you receive 1 credit for a book to be sent to you. You also receive 1 credit whenever you mail out a book. You only ever pay for shipping of your books to others, then in return others pay for the shipping of their books to you. No money is ever exchanged, only postage paid to a shipper!

One piece of advice when browsing for a book, always use the Amazon search instead of the bookmooch search, their search takes forever!

Khan Academy

Started by Salman Khan, a MIT and Harvard Business School graduate when he started tutoring his cousins and distributed his tutorials via YouTube. It's a non-profit, and is backed by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and Google, among others.

You can use the main Khan Academy website, which allows you to track progress, has exercises, and tools for teachers; you can just browse their YouTube channel; you can download their iOS app if you have an iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch; or you can download their Windows 8 app if you are using a PC with Windows 8. As far as I know, they don't have an Android or Windows Phone app.

If you wanted to use their website but don't have a Google or Facebook account (or just don't want to use it), you can also create a Khan Academy account. Click on the green "Log In" button in the upper-right, and then in the lower-right of the sign-in screen, you'll see a "Sign up" link in the "Log in with a Khan Academy account" section.
